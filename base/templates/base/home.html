About

Productivity has become an even bigger part of our daily lives. Our app is developed to help you stayed focused and do even more.


Socket gives you the power organized all the small things in your daily routine. Simple to use and in just few seconds to take notes fluidly across all of your devices.


Activate modules that you think works best for you and use them. My Notes which help you to save all task that you don't want to forget or Pomodoro to help you manage your time for focus and ofcourse some rest time.
